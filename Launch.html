The Team

Our Goal

Our goal is to educate and mentor young men of color.

WHY?

Many lack role models in their life, Older men do not feel a strong responsibility for mentoring the youth outside of their respective communities, and many do not understand the importance of mentoring.

There will be schedualed programs, guides, and events.
